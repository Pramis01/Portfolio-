Design Project

Logo Design

Logo Design

Created a custom logo for the Instagram page Quote with Nature, combining natural elements with a modern aesthetic. The logo features green as the primary color to represent nature, with black as a complementary tone to add balance and depth. The design includes an elephant representing the letter "E" and a tree symbolizing "T" in the word "nature." Product Design & Branding

Branding, Product Photography Visual Design

Product Design & Branding

For this project, I designed promotional content for an accessories brand, Elegance Accessories. I created two different formats: one for a standard Instagram post (portrait) and another optimized for Instagram Story. I photographed the pearl rings myself and used Adobe Illustrator to design the layout and create the brand logo. The design focuses on simplicity, luxury, and clarity — using bold typography, neutral tones, and high-quality visuals to highlight the product. The “Pre-Order” button gives it a real e-commerce feel, and the two-format approach shows how the brand could consistently present itself across social media.
